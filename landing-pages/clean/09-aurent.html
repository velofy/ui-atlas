The making

Three convictions, cut into every piece.

  1. 01

    One movement, one roof

    The AUR-01 calibre is drawn, cut, and assembled in a single workshop, then adjusted in five positions across seventy-two hours of running reserve. Nothing is outsourced, so nothing is anonymous.

  2. 02

    Engraved, never printed

    Each dial passes by hand under a century-old rose engine. The guilloche you see is cut into solid metal, not laid on top of it. No two dials carry the same line, and none can be repeated.

  3. 03

    Numbered, and cared for

    We finish fewer than two hundred watches a year. Every piece is numbered, entered in the atelier ledger, and welcomed back for service for as long as it runs. A watch you buy once.

We would rather build one watch slowly than a thousand quickly.

Elise Aurent, watchmaker · Northern Jura
