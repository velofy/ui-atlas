Why Arcade9

An arcade, not a slot machine.

Every rule of the storefront was rewritten around one idea: a small, curated floor beats an infinite one.

01

Hand-picked, never ranked

There is no algorithm deciding what floats to the top, because there is no top. A rotating floor of nine cabinets, each one played end to end by our curators before it goes live. You browse a room, not a feed.

02

Developers keep 92 percent

A flat 8 percent platform fee, no ad buys, no featured-slot auctions, no fine print. Payouts land every two weeks. The people who make the games keep what the games earn.

03

Every game is playable first

Each cabinet ships a real demo that runs in your browser: no download, no account, no wallet. Play the first level, then decide if it is worth your coin. The first demo is always on the house.

How the floor works

Three steps from submission to spotlight.

Placement is never for sale. A cabinet is earned by being played, not bought with an ad spend.

Step 01

We play the whole thing

Every submission is played end to end by a curator, not scanned by a bot. If it does not hold up, it does not ship.

Step 02

It earns a cabinet

Nine games make the floor each week. A cabinet is a spot in the room, not a slot you can buy. Placement is never for sale.

Step 03

You try before you pay

Pull the joystick on the in-browser demo. Like it, drop a coin, and 92 percent goes straight to the developer.
