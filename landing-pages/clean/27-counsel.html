The platform

One authoritative record, from request to signature.

Legal has been run out of email threads and spreadsheets for too long. Counsel gives your team a defensible operating system: everything routed, versioned, and provable.

Intake that routes itself

Every request enters through one door, gets classified, and lands with the right owner in minutes. Retire the legal@ alias and the "who owns this?" thread for good.
