FAQ

The short version.

Does it train on my data?

No. Your content is never used to train a model. It stays inside your workspace, and you can export or delete all of it at any time.

What can I attach?

PDFs, spreadsheets, documents, and images. Cortex works from the source file and cites the specific line or cell it used, so you can check its work.

Is the web always on?

No. Web access is off by default. Flip the globe on for a request when you want live sources, and Cortex will tell you which pages it read.

Do I need to set anything up?

No card and no configuration. Type your first request in the box above and Cortex gets to work.
