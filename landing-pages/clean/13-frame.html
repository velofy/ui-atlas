Why Frame

Built on one idea: get out of the way.

Most portfolio builders treat a photograph like content to be surrounded. Frame is the argument against the surround.

One frame, fully lit

Full bleed, edge to edge, one caption. No thumbnail rail crowding the margins, no autoplay carousel deciding your pace. The image gets the whole screen because you earned it the whole screen.

Sequenced like a gallery wall

Arrange your set as a contact sheet, then let visitors walk it in the order you chose. Frame keeps your pacing intact, from the opening frame to the one you held back for last.
