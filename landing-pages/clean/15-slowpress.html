The long way round

Three steps, none of them rushed.

STEP 01

Source by hand

We buy small lots directly from growers we visit, paying above market for the pick that is worth roasting. If it will not cup clean, we pass.

STEP 02

Roast to order

Orders close at midnight. By morning the drum is warm, each profile dialled by taste, not a timer. Nothing roasts until someone is waiting for it.

STEP 03

Ship the same day

Beans rest a few hours to release gas, then go out in recyclable kraft with a one-way valve and the roast date stamped on the seam.
