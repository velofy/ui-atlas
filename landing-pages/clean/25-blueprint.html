How we work / 01 to 03

Three rules we do not bend

01 / METHOD

Section first, plan second

We design the way a building is built: from how it meets the ground to how it meets the sky. The plan follows the section, never the reverse.

02 / METHOD

Detail before image

No mood board survives contact with a threshold. We draw the reveal, the drip, the flashing at 1:5 before we render a single view.

03 / METHOD

One set of truth

Architecture, structure and services are coordinated on the same sheet. The contractor bids what is drawn, and builds exactly what was bid.

Studio principle No. 3

Drawing is not the documentation of a decision already made. Drawing is where the decision gets made.

Posted above every desk in the studio, Rua da Graca

What handing us a site looks like

PH. 01 Survey and section studies.We measure the site, the light and the neighbours, then test the idea in three sections before a plan exists. 2 to 4 weeks
PH. 02 Developed design.Structure, envelope and services are drawn together. You approve real details, not a hopeful picture. 6 to 10 weeks
PH. 03 Tender documentation.The full coordinated set goes out. Contractors price certainty instead of padding for ambiguity. 4 to 8 weeks
PH. 04 On site.We answer to the drawing. Weekly on the ground, we hold the built work to the lines we committed to. On site
