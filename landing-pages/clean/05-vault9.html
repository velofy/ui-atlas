Why Vault9

Custody with no one in the middle.

The whole point of crypto was to remove the trusted third party. Vault9 removes it from your wallet too.

0x01

Keys that never leave

Your 24-word recovery phrase is generated offline and sealed in your device secure enclave. Our servers never receive it, so there is nothing on our side to leak, subpoena, or misplace.

0x02

Sign exactly what you see

Every transaction is decoded into plain terms before it broadcasts: amount, destination, and network fee, in full. You approve the details in front of you, never a blind signature.

0x03

Recover without a middleman

Split your backup into five shards kept by people and places you choose. Restore from any three. No custodian holds a copy, and no single loss can lock you out.

How keys stay yours

Three steps, zero custody.

Nothing about your keys ever depends on our uptime, our goodwill, or our servers staying online.

Step 01

Generate offline

A 24-word seed is created on-device from hardware entropy. It never touches the network.

Step 02

Encrypt on device

Keys are wrapped inside the secure enclave, unlocked only by your passcode or biometrics.

Step 03

Sign locally

Transactions are signed on your device, then broadcast as read-only data. Your keys stay put.

// design principle
