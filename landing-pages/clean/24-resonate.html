Why it sounds different

Built for people who still listen to albums front to back.

Three decisions shape everything about Resonate. Each one is a stance, not a setting buried in a menu.

Master quality, by default

Every track streams in 24-bit lossless, the same file the mastering engineer signed off on. It is not a premium tier or a hidden toggle. Your ears earned it.

No compression

Artists keep 70 percent

We take a flat, published cut and pass the rest straight through. Follow a band and you can see, to the cent, what your listens paid them this month.

Albums, not slot machines
