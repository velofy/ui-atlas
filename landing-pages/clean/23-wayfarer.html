What Wayfarer does

Built for people who would rather wander well than tick boxes.

Most planners optimize for the shortest line between bookings. Wayfarer optimizes for the day you actually want to have.

Routes that breathe

We pace each day around how far you can actually walk and when places open, so you are never sprinting between two pins on opposite ends of town.

One map, every layer

Stays, food, trails, and roadside curiosities all live on a single map. Drag a pin to a different day and the whole route redraws itself.

Offline by default

Download a region before you go. Your route, notes, and pins keep working in the mountains, on the night train, anywhere the signal drops.
