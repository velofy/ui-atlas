The practice

We hold three rules, and they cost us most of the calendar.

01

Made to order

Nothing exists until you ask for it. We take your measurements, cut a single pattern, and hand you a six week wait that is the entire point. Fast is somebody else's promise.

02

Honest materials

Undyed wool, boiled linen, horn and corozo. Fibers that age into character instead of out of fashion, and nothing on the garment we cannot name on the label.

03

Mended for life

Bring it back frayed and we reline, reweave, and return it. The first repair is on us, and so is every conversation about the next ten years of wear.
