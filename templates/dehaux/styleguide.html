Combinations that hold

Field notes
Copper, sage, bonePair the copper accent with sage status pills and bone ink. This is the default, and it holds without effort.
Brass runs deeperBrass likes deeper shadows and a hush; keep the callout sparse and let the gilt do the talking.
Verdigris with oliveVerdigris reads best with olive status. Never mix two accent palettes on one screen.
Italic serif is spoken forThe italic serif is always the accent color, never a second hue.
